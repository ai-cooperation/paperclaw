Dear Editor-in-Chief Dr. Robert G. Eccles,

Please find enclosed our manuscript entitled “LLM-Assisted ESG Disclosure Assessment for Taiwan Listed Companies” submitted for consideration in *Business Strategy and the Environment*. This paper presents a novel large language model (LLM)-assisted framework designed to improve the accuracy and consistency of ESG disclosure assessments for companies listed on the Taiwan Stock Exchange. By leveraging domain-specific fine-tuning and interpretability enhancements, our approach addresses the challenges of conventional manual and rule-based scoring methods, which often suffer from limited scalability and subjective variability.

Our study’s three key contributions are as follows:
1. We develop a tailored LLM-based ESG disclosure assessment framework that incorporates Taiwan’s unique linguistic, regulatory, and disclosure contexts, improving relevance and applicability.
2. Through extensive empirical evaluation using a dataset of ESG reports from Taiwan-listed firms, we demonstrate significant improvements in scoring accuracy and inter-rater reliability compared to existing manual and automated methods.
3. We provide a scalable, interpretable tool that offers actionable insights for investors, regulators, and companies, fostering transparency and enhancing sustainable investment decision-making in Taiwan’s market.

We believe *Business Strategy and the Environment* is the ideal venue for this work due to its interdisciplinary scope at the nexus of business strategy and sustainability, its emphasis on empirical and innovative methodological contributions, and its global readership interested in ESG disclosure and corporate sustainability issues. Our study also complements the journal’s focus on advancing transparent, data-driven sustainability assessments in diverse regional contexts.

Data supporting our findings are available upon reasonable request consistent with data sharing agreements.
