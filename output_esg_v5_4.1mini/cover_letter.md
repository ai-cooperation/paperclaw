Dear Editor-in-Chief,

Please consider our manuscript entitled “LLM-Assisted ESG Disclosure Assessment for Taiwan Listed Companies” for publication in *Business Strategy and the Environment*. This paper presents a novel large language model (LLM)-based analytic framework designed to enhance the accuracy, efficiency, and comprehensiveness of Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) disclosure assessments specifically for Taiwan-listed companies, addressing both Mandarin and English disclosures in their unique regulatory and cultural context.

Our study makes three key contributions: (1) the development of an LLM framework fine-tuned for bilingual ESG disclosure interpretation, incorporating ESG-specific ontologies tailored to Taiwan’s market; (2) an empirical evaluation of ESG disclosure quality and patterns across multiple sectors within Taiwan, offering new insights into corporate sustainability reporting in a region underserved by existing literature; and (3) the delivery of a scalable automated tool that supports investors, regulators, and other stakeholders in monitoring ESG practices more effectively, promoting transparency and strategic sustainability decisions.

We believe *Business Strategy and the Environment* is the ideal forum for this research, given the journal’s focus on innovative approaches linking business strategy with environmental and social sustainability challenges. Our integration of cutting-edge AI technology with corporate ESG assessment aligns with the journal’s mission to advance sustainable business practices worldwide, and will be of interest to your international readership comprising academics, practitioners, and policymakers.

All underlying data supporting our findings are available within the manuscript and its supplementary materials.
