# Research Concept — LLM-Assisted ESG Disclosure Assessment for Taiwan Listed Companies

# Research Concept Document

## 1. Research Question
How effectively can large language models (LLMs) assist in assessing Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) disclosures of Taiwan-listed companies compared to traditional manual and automated methods?

## 2. Testable Hypothesis
LLM-assisted assessment of ESG disclosures for Taiwan-listed companies provides higher accuracy, consistency, and efficiency than existing manual evaluations and rule-based automated tools.

## 3. Contributions

### Technical Contribution
- Development and fine-tuning of LLM-based NLP models specifically tailored to extract, interpret, and score ESG disclosures in Mandarin and English from Taiwan-listed companies’ reports, addressing linguistic and contextual nuances.

### Empirical Contribution
- Comprehensive evaluation and benchmarking of LLM-assisted ESG assessment against traditional manual coding and existing automated methods using a dataset of ESG disclosures from Taiwan’s stock market, providing robust performance metrics.

### Practical Contribution
- Provision of an actionable framework and toolkit for investors, regulators, and corporate managers in Taiwan to leverage AI-driven ESG assessment for improved transparency, decision-making, and regulatory compliance.

## 4. Limitations in Existing Literature (Research Gaps)

1. **Language and Regional Focus:** Most ESG disclosure assessment research using LLMs focuses on English-language reports from Western markets; limited work exists on Taiwan-listed companies, which present unique linguistic and regulatory characteristics.
2. **Methodological Rigor:** Existing automated ESG assessments often rely on keyword-based or rule-based approaches with limited semantic understanding, resulting in inconsistencies and reduced accuracy.
3. **Integration of Large Language Models:** There is a lack of studies exploring the specific use and calibration of advanced LLMs (e.g., GPT variants) in the context of ESG disclosure analysis, particularly within Asian emerging markets such as Taiwan.

## 5. Suggested Target Journal

**Business Strategy and the Environment**

### Rationale:
This journal is a leading interdisciplinary platform focused on organizational strategies for environmental and social sustainability, making it highly suitable for research that combines cutting-edge AI methodologies with ESG disclosure and sustainability assessment. Its readership includes academics, policymakers, and practitioners engaged in sustainability-related business strategy, aligning well with the practical and empirical impacts of LLM-assisted ESG evaluation in Taiwan.
