Dear Editor-in-Chief,

We are pleased to submit our manuscript entitled **“LLM-Assisted ESG Disclosure Assessment for Taiwan Listed Companies”** for consideration for publication in **Business Strategy and the Environment**.

This paper examines whether large language models (LLMs) can more accurately and consistently assess ESG disclosure quality in annual and sustainability reports of Taiwan-listed companies than conventional keyword-based or rule-based methods. By focusing on Chinese and bilingual corporate reporting, the study evaluates semantic completeness, specificity, and relevance in ESG narratives.

Our study makes three key contributions. First, it develops an LLM-assisted framework for ESG disclosure assessment that moves beyond surface-level text counting. Second, it provides systematic evidence on ESG disclosure quality across Taiwan-listed firms and industries. Third, it offers a scalable and auditable tool with practical value for regulators, investors, and firms seeking to benchmark and improve sustainability reporting.

We believe **Business Strategy and the Environment** is an excellent fit because the manuscript addresses sustainability reporting, ESG transparency, and corporate strategy while also introducing an innovative AI-based method with strong managerial and policy relevance.

**Data availability statement:** The data used in this study are derived from publicly available annual reports and sustainability reports of Taiwan-listed companies. Any additional data, coding procedures, or replication materials will be made available upon reasonable request.
