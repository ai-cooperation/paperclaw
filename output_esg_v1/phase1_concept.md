# Research Concept — LLM-Assisted ESG Disclosure Assessment for Taiwan Listed Companies

# Research Concept Document

## 1. Research Question
How effectively can large language models (LLMs) assist in assessing the ESG disclosures of Taiwan-listed companies, and what impact does their use have on the accuracy, consistency, and timeliness of ESG reporting evaluation?

## 2. Hypothesis
LLM-assisted ESG disclosure assessment significantly improves the accuracy, consistency, and timeliness of evaluating ESG reports of Taiwan-listed companies compared to traditional manual assessment methods.

## 3. Contributions

### Technical Contribution
- Development and validation of an LLM-based framework tailored for analyzing ESG disclosures specific to Taiwan’s regulatory and market context, incorporating local language nuances and sector-specific ESG terminology.

### Empirical Contribution
- Empirical evaluation comparing LLM-assisted assessments with conventional analyst evaluations on a dataset of Taiwan-listed companies’ ESG reports, providing quantitative evidence on performance improvements.

### Practical Contribution
- Insights and guidelines for corporate sustainability officers, investors, and regulators on integrating AI tools like LLMs to enhance ESG disclosure assessment processes, potentially driving better transparency and informed investment decisions.

## 4. Limitations in Existing Literature (Research Gaps)
- **Gap 1:** Limited research on the application of LLMs for ESG disclosure assessment in emerging Asian markets, particularly Taiwan, where ESG reporting practices and regulatory requirements differ from Western contexts.
- **Gap 2:** Insufficient empirical studies evaluating the accuracy and reliability of AI-assisted ESG analysis compared to traditional human analyst methods.
- **Gap 3:** Lack of exploration into practical challenges and user acceptance issues when deploying AI tools for ESG assessment within corporate governance and financial analysis workflows.

## 5. Suggested Target Journal
**Business Strategy and the Environment**

**Rationale:**
- The journal focuses on the intersection of business strategy, environmental sustainability, and innovative approaches to sustainability challenges.
- It has a strong interest in empirical and applied research related to ESG disclosure and corporate sustainability practices.
- The integration of cutting-edge AI techniques like LLMs in sustainability assessment aligns well with the journal’s scope of advancing knowledge on strategic tools for environmental and social governance.
